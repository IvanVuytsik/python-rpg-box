Strength influences skills requiring raw physical power
Constitution determines your health and resistances
Dexterity influences skills requiring swift hands
Agility influences skills requiring quick reaction and mobility
Awareness influences skills requiring attention to detail and observation
Personality influences skills requiring interaction with living creatures
Intelligence influences skills based on manipulation of information
Wisdom determines your ability to see a bigger picture and learn
Willpower determines your resistances and perseverance
Luck influences favorable events and affects many skills
